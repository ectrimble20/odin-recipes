Baked Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Garlic Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 425 Degrees F
  2. Prepare chicken, removing excess fat and tentions
  3. Coat chicken with olive oil and Garlic Pepper, flip and repeat
  4. Prepare pan, line using foil, coat foil in olive oil
  5. Bake for 25 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
