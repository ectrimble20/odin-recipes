Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Garlic Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare Pan, coat with cooking oil and set heat to low/medium, higher if you want to cook em real fast
  2. Crack 2 or 3 eggs into a glass, using a fork or a wisk scramble those suckers into a nice egg mess
  3. Add about 1/4 a cup of milk and wisk some more
  4. Add more milk if desired, the more milk the fluffier they tend to be, but you can go over board and make a soupy mess
  5. Pour eggs into pan
  6. Add about 1/8 to 1/4 a tea spoon of garlic salt
  7. Pepper to your liking
  8. Add cheese as you see fit
  9. Using a spatula, move the eggs around so they cook through
  10. Put them on a plate and eat them.
