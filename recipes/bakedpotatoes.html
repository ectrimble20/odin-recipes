Baked Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 450 Degrees F
  2. Wash Potatoes
  3. Prepare pan, line using foil, coat foil in olive oil
  4. using sharp knife or fork, poke holes all over the potatoes, both sides
  5. Coat potatoes using olive oil
  6. Rub salt on potatoes
  7. Bake for 20 minutes
  8. Flip potatoes
  9. Bake for 25 minutes
  10. Enjoy!
